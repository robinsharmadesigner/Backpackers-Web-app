Plan Your Trip

If you’re going to do Europe, do it right. As in, travel for 7 days across 6 cities on our biggest and baddest Europe trip ever. As you learn to conquer the metro system in Paris, flood your Instagram with everything from Big Ben to the Colosseum to the picturesque beaches of Greece, this Europe trip is more informational and awesome than any history lesson your college professor could give you. And, it’s as fast-paced and epic as backpacking— minus the blisters, plus a real bed. This trip will prove that things in the U.S. aren’t that old, maps are confusing, espresso is amazing, and travel is always the answer. This is Ultimate Europe.
