Plan Your Trip

Gazing out over Cape Town from Table Mountain, the panorama alone is worth the trip to southern Africa. Then again, this epic 5-day adventure to South Africa and Namibia is filled with rewards for travellers. Marvel at the Cape Floral Kingdom on stunning display at the Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden, watch for the "big five" in Kruger National Park, and climb dunes in the sweeping deserts of Namibia. Along the way, the views just may change your outlook.
